ENTREVAGANZA

Odd semester fest

Onyx, the Entrepreneurship Development Cell of The National Institute of Engineering, Mysore inaugurated the “Entrevaganza 2015” on 31st October, 2015. The event was formally inaugurated by the chief guest Mr.Sumanth Prabhu, founder of Vesto Education Solution who recently conducted Digital Dasara Exhibition in Mysore; Vesto has also has been a recipient of many awards. The inauguration function was presided by Dr.G.L.Shekar (Principal, NIE), Mr.S.K.Lakshminarayana (Secretary, NIE), Dr.M.V.Achutha (Professor, Mechanical Department), Mr.Rajaram (Director, KBITS) and Mr.Yogesh M J (Faculty Coordinator).

E-tour
The basic ideology behind E-tour was to enlighten the students about the journey of entrepreneurship,the problems they face to have a successful startup,and finally how a successful startup works.The Entrepreneurial Tour had its visits at KAYPEEYES BIOTECH PRIVATE LIMITED and THE HOBBY PLACE.Kaypeeyes Biotech is a completely flourished startup which was started in the year 1993.The Hobby Place was started just a few years back.

KEYNOTE SPEECH
A keynote speech for the national level b-plan presentation competetion was delivered by Ms.Mala Mary Martina,Founder and CEO at I LOVE MONDAYS and also the author of the book "I LOVE MONDAYS” on the 1st of November,2015 at The National Institute Of Engineering,Mysore .She is known for her sheer experience in being an entrepreneur and as an expert orator.Ms.Martina started the session by sharing with the audience , the key points for becoming an entrepreneur which included her personal life instances and then was open for questions.She shared her experience of starting I LOVE MONDAYS and the problems she faced as she was from the engineering background.She advised the audience to follow their dreams and strive hard to reach their dreams. The session finally came to an end .Ms.Martina successfully catered the spirit of entrepreneurship into the hearts of the audience through this interactive session.

Burnout
The Burnout is a new game designed by the members of ONYX. The game is based on the principles of team building, business tactics, negotiations and creativity which are highly essential in shaping a successful entrepreneurial journey. Students from various colleges participated in the contest.go on with the acquisition. Several institutions of the day to day world such as banks, workers' union, middle men, police and courts had been set up to cater the game. The teams had to come up with a solution which could take them out of the problem and negotiate with the respective institutions to save their company. This helped them to realize the troubles faced in running a business and the reality of the various government and non-government institutions. It made them understand how various institutions work and what is necessary to deal with them.

Etalk
On the lush green lawns of NIE was delivered an inspirational talk on entrepreneurship by Mr Ashwin Dhange, a budding entrepreneur and founder the startups Sweet-n-Swirl ice-creams and Digipulse. Mr Dhange, himself being an alumnus of NIE and one of the founder members of our e-cell Onyx, had many stories to inspire. His ups and downs, joys and sorrows,nightmares and pleasant moments, every piece of his talk was inspiring and it left an ever lasting impression in the hearts of the students. He advised five major principles which have been very helpful to him in establishing himself as a successful entrepreneur.
